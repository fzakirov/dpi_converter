Supported Formats

We currently accept PDF and SVG files as inputs. These vector formats are perfectly scaled into ultra-high resolution PNG or JPG output images (targeted at 6000px width), making them ideal for academic publications and posters.

About

FigureGen simplifies the process of creating publishing-ready figures without needing a subscription to complex design tools. The backend utilizes native python Cairo and Poppler libraries to guarantee zero loss of fidelity during conversion.
